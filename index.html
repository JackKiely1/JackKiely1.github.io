--- layout: home title: Lost & Found Mobile App subtitle: A campus mobile app for reporting, searching for, and recovering lost items --- The Lost & Found Mobile App is a final year project that improves how lost property is managed on a college campus. It provides a central place where users can report lost items, upload found items, browse listings, and support faster recovery of personal belongings. ### What the project does - Allows users to report lost items - Allows users to upload found items - Makes it easier to browse and recover belongings on campus ### Explore the project - [About the Project](/aboutme/) - [Features](/features/) - [Project Poster](/poster/) - [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/JackKiely1/JackKiely1.github.io)
