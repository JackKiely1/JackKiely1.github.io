## App Features

### Lost item reporting
Users can submit details about an item they have lost, including description, category, and location.

### Found item reporting
Users can upload information about items they have found on campus.

### Browsing and searching
Users can browse available listings and search for items more easily.

### Centralised campus platform
The system provides one organised place for lost-and-found activity instead of relying on informal communication.

### Improved recovery process
By increasing visibility and accessibility, the app improves the chances of reuniting users with their belongings.
