<div style="max-width: 1000px; margin: 0 auto;">

  <div style="text-align: center; margin-bottom: 40px;">
    <p style="font-size: 1.2rem; line-height: 1.8;">
      The Lost &amp; Found Mobile App is a final year project designed to improve how lost property is managed on a college campus. It provides a central place where users can report lost items, upload found items, browse listings, and support faster recovery of personal belongings.
    </p>
  </div>

  <h2 style="text-align: center; margin-bottom: 25px;">What the Project Does</h2>

  <div style="display: flex; flex-wrap: wrap; gap: 20px; justify-content: center; margin-bottom: 50px;">
    <div style="flex: 1 1 250px; max-width: 280px; background: #f5f5f5; padding: 20px; border-radius: 12px; text-align: center;">
      <h3>Report Items</h3>
      <p>Users can quickly report lost or found items through a simple mobile interface.</p>
    </div>

    <div style="flex: 1 1 250px; max-width: 280px; background: #f5f5f5; padding: 20px; border-radius: 12px; text-align: center;">
      <h3>Browse Listings</h3>
      <p>Students and staff can browse available listings to check if an item has been reported.</p>
    </div>

    <div style="flex: 1 1 250px; max-width: 280px; background: #f5f5f5; padding: 20px; border-radius: 12px; text-align: center;">
      <h3>Improve Recovery</h3>
      <p>The platform improves visibility, communication, and the overall recovery process on campus.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
