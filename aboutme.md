<h2 style="text-align:center; margin-bottom:40px; color:#435465;">About the Project</h2>

<div style="display:flex; flex-wrap:wrap; gap:20px; justify-content:center; margin-bottom:60px;">

<div style="flex:1 1 420px; background:#F4F7F9; border-top:4px solid #378B84; padding:25px; border-radius:12px; box-shadow:0 3px 10px rgba(67,84,101,0.08);">
<h3 style="color:#435465;">Problem</h3>
<p style="color:#435465;">
Students regularly lose personal belongings such as keys, wallets, earphones, chargers, and student cards. Traditional lost-and-found systems are often inconsistent, difficult to access, or dependent on word of mouth.
</p>
</div>

<div style="flex:1 1 420px; background:#F4F7F9; border-top:4px solid #0062AF; padding:25px; border-radius:12px; box-shadow:0 3px 10px rgba(67,84,101,0.08);">
<h3 style="color:#435465;">Solution</h3>
<p style="color:#435465;">
This project introduces a mobile application that creates a centralised and more organised process for reporting lost items and sharing found item information.
</p>
</div>

<div style="flex:1 1 420px; background:#F4F7F9; border-top:4px solid #FCCA3A; padding:25px; border-radius:12px; box-shadow:0 3px 10px rgba(67,84,101,0.08);">
<h3 style="color:#435465;">How it Works</h3>
<p style="color:#435465;">
Users can submit a lost item report or create a found item post. Other users can browse listings and identify possible matches based on item details, category, and location.
</p>
</div>

<div style="flex:1 1 420px; background:#F4F7F9; border-top:4px solid #378B84; padding:25px; border-radius:12px; box-shadow:0 3px 10px rgba(67,84,101,0.08);">
<h3 style="color:#435465;">Project Aim</h3>
<p style="color:#435465;">
The aim of the project is to create a practical campus-based mobile solution that improves visibility, communication, and recovery outcomes for lost belongings.
</p>
</div>
